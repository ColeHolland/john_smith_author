September 29, 2016

Today had this crazy dream. I had i dream that I had to deliver a straw to someone in Jordan. My only method of transportation was a bouncy house. I was bouncing my way to Jordan, but I had to go to the grocery store to buy cream cheese. As I was bouncing, my bouncy house deflated. It was flying all over the place. I had to outrun the deflated bouncy in thirty seconds, or else my mission to get the straw to Jordan failed. I was able to outrun the deflated bouncy house, and then I found myself in New York City. I accidentally dropped my straw in a hill filled with garbage and lost it. I asked the garbage man where my straw was, but the dream ended before he had a chance to answer. I told my sister about this dream and she thought I was on crack.
